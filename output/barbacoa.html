the whole clove
recipes
mains

barbacoa

Special Equipment

  • dutch oven

Ingredients

  • 2lbs chuck roast
  • 1 sweet onion
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 chipotle chilis
  • 5 chiles de arbol
  • smoked paprika
  • 4C beef stock
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cumin
  • msg
  • oregano
  • chili powder

Directions

  1. Heat dutch oven over medium-high heat.

  2. Cut beef into pieces if needed. Salt and sear in hot pan for 2-3 minutes per side until a brown crust forms.

    2lbs chuck roast, salt

  3. Remove beef. Add onions, garlic, peppers, and spices. Cook until fragrant. Add beef stock.

    1 sweet onion, 5 cloves garlic, 2 chipotle chilis, 5 chiles de arbol, smoked paprika, 4C beef stock, pepper, cumin, msg, oregano, chili powder
  4. Add beef back to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  5. Simmer for several hours until beef is very tender and shreds easily.

Notes

  • I used 3 lbs of meat and 1.5x everything. Beef stock preferred. Used around 3g of every spice but could use more.
  • Can bake in oven at 375°F.
